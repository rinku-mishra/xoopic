693 /**
694  * Hydrogen Monte Carlo Tunneling Ionization (HMCTI) class.
695  * Specializes the MCTIPackage class for the simulations
696  * of tunneling ionization of Hydrogen under the influence
697  * of linearly polarized alternating electric field.
698  *
699  * This class implements the formula for the probability
700  * rate for tunneling ionization from:
701  * N. B. Delone and V. P. Krainov, "Multiphonon Processes
702  * in Atoms", Springer (2000), Eq. (4.6) on page 71 with
703  * quantum numbers (n, l, m) = (1, 0, 0), i.e. only for
704  * tunneling ionization from the ground state.
705  * This formula is expected to be applicable only for electric
706  * field magnitudes: E << E_atomic = 1/(16n^4) for Hydrogen.
707  * The condition for tunneling ionization to occur is:
708  * gamma^2 << 1, where gamma = omega*sqrt(2*E_i)/E,
709  * omega is the frequency of the alternating electric
710  * field with magnitude E, and E_i is the magnitude
711  * of the bound state energy from which the electron is
712  * to tunnel. Both linearly and circularly polarized
713  * fields are handled.
714  *
715  * First added March, 2001.
716  *
717  * @author D. A. Dimitrov
718  */

762  * and is the same as the TI probability for this state in
763  * static E field.
764  */

766  return ((4.0/E)*exp(-2.0/(3.0*E))*dt);

771  * step dt (both in atomic units).
772  */
