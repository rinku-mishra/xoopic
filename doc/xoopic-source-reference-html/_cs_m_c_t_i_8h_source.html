1 /**
2  * Cs Monte Carlo Tunneling Ionization (CsMCTI) class.
3  * Specializes the MCTIPackage class for the simulations
4  * of tunneling ionization of Cs under the influence
5  * of linearly/circularly polarized alternating electric field.
6  *
7  * This class implements the formula for the probability
8  * rate for tunneling ionization from ground states, see e.g.:
9  * N. B. Delone and V. P. Krainov, "Multiphonon Processes
10  * in Atoms", Springer (2000).
11  * This formula is expected to be applicable only for electric
12  * field magnitudes: E << E_atomic = Z/(16n^4).
13  * The adiabatic approximation condition for tunneling is:
14  * omega << E_i and
15  * gamma^2 << 1, where gamma = omega*sqrt(2*E_i)/E,
16  * omega is the frequency of the alternating electric
17  * field with magnitude E, and E_i is the magnitude
18  * of the bound state energy from which the electron is
19  * to tunnel.
20  *
21  * First added January, 2003.
22  *
23  * @author D. A. Dimitrov
24  */
