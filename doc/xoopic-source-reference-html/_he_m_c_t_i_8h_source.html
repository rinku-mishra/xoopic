1 /**
2  * He Monte Carlo Tunneling Ionization (HeMCTI) class.
3  * Specializes the MCTIPackage class for the simulations
4  * of tunneling ionization of He under the influence
5  * of linearly polarized alternating electric field.
6  *
7  * This class implements the formula for the probability
8  * rate for tunneling ionization from:
9  * N. B. Delone and V. P. Krainov, "Multiphonon Processes
10  * in Atoms", Springer (2000), Eq. (4.6) on page 71 with
11  * quantum numbers (n, l, m) = (1, 0, 0), i.e. only for
12  * tunneling ionization from the ground state.
13  * This formula is expected to be applicable only for electric
14  * field magnitudes: E << E_atomic = Z/(16n^4).
15  * The adiabatic approximation condition for tunneling is:
16  * omega << E_i and
17  * gamma^2 << 1, where gamma = omega*sqrt(2*E_i)/E,
18  * omega is the frequency of the alternating electric
19  * field with magnitude E, and E_i is the magnitude
20  * of the bound state energy from which the electron is
21  * to tunnel.
22  *
23  * First added June, 2001.
24  *
25  * @author D. A. Dimitrov
26  */
