4 PARTICLEGROUP.H
5 
6  This class defines a group of particles which are related by a common
7  charge to mass ratio and a common computational particle weight. The
8  ParticleGroup class reduces storage of redundant values of charge,
9  mass and particle weight, and improves performance in the critical
10  particle push loop. Variable weighting allows q[] to vary, while
11  holding np2c0 fixed at the maximum weighting for the group.
12

19 1.1 (JohnV 01-09-97) Add energy_eV().
